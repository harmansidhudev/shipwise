# Legal Compliance (Final)

> Phase 3: SHIP | Sprint 4 (planned)
> Disclaimer: Organizational checklists, not legal advice. Consult qualified professionals.

## Coverage

- Privacy policy (data collected, purpose, retention, sharing, jurisdiction)
- Terms of service (acceptable use, liability, IP, disputes, termination)
- Cookie consent (granular opt-in, analytics vs marketing, DNT)
- GDPR/CCPA (DSARs, right to deletion, portability, consent records)
- Data retention policy (define per data type, automate purging)
- SOC 2 readiness (B2B: policies, access controls, audit logging)
- Open source license audit (inventory deps, GPL caution)

## Checklist Items

### Privacy Policy
<!-- beginner -->
**Create a privacy policy** — Required by law in most jurisdictions if you collect ANY user data (including email addresses and analytics). It must say what data you collect, why, who you share it with, and how users can delete their data. Use a generator to start, then customize.

<!-- intermediate -->
**Privacy policy** — Data inventory, purpose, retention periods, third-party sharing, DSAR process. Cover GDPR + CCPA if applicable.

<!-- senior -->
**Privacy policy** — Data map, legal basis, retention, DSAR, jurisdiction-specific.
