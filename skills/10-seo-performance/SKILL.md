## Checklist Items

### Meta Tags
<!-- beginner -->
**Add meta tags to every page** — Meta tags tell search engines and social media what your page is about. Without them, Google shows random text and shared links look broken on Twitter/LinkedIn. Every page needs: title, description, Open Graph image.

<!-- intermediate -->
**Meta tags + OG** — Title, description, og:title, og:description, og:image per page. Twitter Cards. Canonical URLs.

<!-- senior -->
**Meta/OG/canonical** — Per-page with Twitter Cards.
